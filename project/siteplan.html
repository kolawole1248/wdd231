Overview

Purpose

The purpose of RecipeRadar is to provide a dynamic meal-planning platform that helps users discover recipes tailored to their dietary needs and preferences. Our website will offer an intuitive interface for searching, saving, and sharing recipes while maintaining a mobile-first approach to ensure accessibility across all devices.

Audience

Our target audience includes busy professionals seeking quick meal solutions, health-conscious individuals with specific dietary requirements, families looking for kid-friendly recipes, and cooking enthusiasts wanting to share their creations. The website will feature clear navigation, responsive design, and visually appealing recipe presentations with a color scheme that evokes freshness and appetite appeal.
